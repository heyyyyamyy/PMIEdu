Our Certifications

Comprehensive certification programs designed to advance your project management career

Project Management Professional (PMP)®

The gold standard in project management certification, recognized globally by employers and organizations.

Most Popular 35 PDUs Required
Certification Overview

The PMP certification demonstrates your competence to perform in the role of a project manager, leading and directing projects and teams.

Prerequisites:
  • Secondary degree with 7,500 hours of project management experience
  • OR Four-year degree with 4,500 hours of project management experience
  • 35 hours of project management education
Exam Details:
  • 180 questions in 230 minutes
  • Multiple choice, multiple responses, matching, hotspot, and limited fill-in-the-blank
  • Three domains: People (42%), Process (50%), Business Environment (8%)
Career Benefits:
  • Average salary increase of 20%
  • Global recognition and credibility
  • Enhanced project management skills
  • Networking opportunities with PMI community
Course Duration: 8 weeks intensive training
Price: $2,499 (includes exam fee)

Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM)®

Perfect entry-level certification for those beginning their project management journey.

Entry Level 23 PDUs Required
Certification Overview

The CAPM certification is designed for project team members and entry-level project managers, or anyone interested in demonstrating their commitment to the project management profession.

Prerequisites:
  • Secondary degree (high school diploma, associate's degree, or global equivalent)
  • 23 hours of project management education completed by the time you sit for the exam
Exam Details:
  • 150 questions in 3 hours
  • Multiple choice format
  • Five domains covering project management fundamentals
Career Benefits:
  • Demonstrates commitment to project management
  • Provides foundation for PMP certification
  • Enhances credibility with employers
  • Opens doors to project management roles
Course Duration: 4 weeks comprehensive training
Price: $1,299 (includes exam fee)

PMI Agile Certified Practitioner (PMI-ACP)®

Validates your ability to use agile practices in your projects while demonstrating your increased professional versatility.

Trending 21 PDUs Required
Certification Overview

The PMI-ACP certification recognizes knowledge of agile principles, practices, and tools and techniques across agile methodologies.

Prerequisites:
  • Secondary degree
  • 2,000 hours of general project experience within the last 5 years
  • 1,500 hours of agile project experience within the last 3 years
  • 21 contact hours of training in agile practices
Exam Details:
  • 120 questions in 3 hours
  • Multiple choice format
  • Covers various agile methodologies including Scrum, Kanban, Lean, XP
Career Benefits:
  • Demonstrates agile expertise to employers
  • Increases earning potential in agile environments
  • Validates knowledge across multiple agile approaches
  • Enhances career opportunities in modern organizations
Course Duration: 6 weeks intensive training
Price: $1,899 (includes exam fee)

PRINCE2® Foundation & Practitioner

Structured project management method widely adopted by UK government and internationally recognized.

Global Standard Dual Level
Certification Overview

PRINCE2 is a process-based method for effective project management, providing a structured approach to managing projects successfully.

Foundation Level:
  • No prerequisites required
  • 60 questions in 60 minutes
  • Multiple choice format
  • Tests knowledge of PRINCE2 terminology and concepts
Practitioner Level:
  • PRINCE2 Foundation certification required
  • 68 questions in 150 minutes
  • Objective testing format
  • Tests ability to apply PRINCE2 in realistic project scenarios
Career Benefits:
  • Widely recognized in Europe and Commonwealth countries
  • Structured methodology for project success
  • Enhances project governance understanding
  • Valuable for government and large enterprise projects
Course Duration: 5 weeks comprehensive training
Price: $1,799 (Foundation + Practitioner bundle)
